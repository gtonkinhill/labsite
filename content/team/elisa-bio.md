Elisa is a visiting PhD student from the University of Zurich, Switzerland, working in [Prof. Adrian Egli’s](https://appliedmicrobiologyresearch.net/en) lab at the Institute of Medical Microbiology. Her project investigates the evolution of antimicrobial resistance, focusing on longitudinal, within-host evolution and *E. coli* strain dynamics. Elisa joined the Tonkin-Hill lab to leverage the team’s tools and expertise to analyse her genomic data and develop a scalable pipeline. Her 6-month research stay is supported by the SNF Mobility grant from the Swiss National Science Foundation and a SEMP stipend by the University of Zurich.
